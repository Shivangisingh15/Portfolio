How I Got Here

I began my journey as a developer with a strong passion for software engineering and programming. After completing my Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) from Microtek, I built a solid foundation in programming and technology, which fueled my desire to create innovative solutions. To deepen my expertise, I pursued a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) from Reva University, where I expanded my knowledge in application development, advanced computing, and software design.

During my time at Galgotias, I engaged in various hands-on projects that allowed me to hone my skills in mobile app development, particularly focusing on Android applications. This academic background not only sharpened my technical capabilities but also strengthened my problem-solving skills, equipping me to tackle complex challenges and deliver impactful software solutions.
